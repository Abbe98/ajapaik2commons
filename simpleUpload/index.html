Mapillary 2 Commons

Ever found a beautiful image on Mapillary and thought That would look great on Wikimedia Commons?

Simply enter the image key or url below and click Check!. Make sure it's the right image then click on the second button to upload the image to Commons, complete with source and attribution.

If Mapillary doesn't know the name of the location you'll be prompted to enter one. You can also pas this page a url directly using the mapillary= url parameter. Don't forget to go to the Commons image afterwards and add categories.
