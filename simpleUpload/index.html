Ajapaik2Commons

Simply enter rephotographed image id below and click Check!. Make sure it's the right image then click on the second button to upload the image to Commons, complete with source and attribution and uploaded using your Wikimedia username.

  • You need a Wikimedia Commons account and to have authorized OAuth Uploader for this tool to work.
  • Note that only rephotographs can be uploaded to commons.
  • Don't forget to go to the Commons image afterwards and add categories.
